Shipping Guide

How to Ship Your Flowers

Proper packaging ensures your blooms arrive in the best condition for preservation.

Keep Flowers Hydrated

Wrap stems in damp paper towels or place in a small bag of water.

Use a Sturdy Box

Pack gently with tissue paper or bubble wrap to prevent crushing.

Ship Within 3 Days

For best results, use 2-day or overnight shipping within 1-3 days of your event.

Include Your Order Info

Add your name, order number, and any special instructions.

Our Story

The Heart Behind
Every Petal

Everbloom was born from a simple moment -- when I pressed the roses from my own wedding bouquet and realized how much beauty could be preserved in a single flower. What started as a personal keepsake became a passion, and then a calling.

Every piece that leaves my studio is crafted entirely by hand. I spend hours with each bouquet, carefully selecting, pressing, and arranging every petal to honor the story it carries.

From wedding bouquets to childhood handprints, from temple art to holiday designs -- each creation is a labor of love, designed to preserve the moments that matter most.

-- Mel, Founder & Artist
