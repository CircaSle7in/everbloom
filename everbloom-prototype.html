Shipping Guide

How to Ship
Your Flowers

Proper packaging ensures your blooms arrive in the best possible condition for preservation. Follow these simple steps.

Keep Flowers Hydrated

Place stems in a small bag of water or wrap in damp paper towels. Freshness matters!

Use a Sturdy Box

Pack flowers gently in a rigid box with tissue paper or bubble wrap to prevent crushing.

Ship Within 3 Days

For best results, ship flowers within 1–3 days of your event. Choose 2-day or overnight shipping.

Include Your Order Info

Tuck a note inside with your name, order number, and any special instructions for your piece.

Our Story

The Heart Behind
Every Petal

Everbloom was born from a simple moment — when I pressed the roses from my own wedding bouquet and realized how much beauty could be preserved in a single flower. What started as a personal keepsake became a passion, and then a calling.

Every piece that leaves my studio is crafted entirely by hand. I spend hours with each bouquet, carefully selecting, pressing, and arranging every petal to honor the story it carries. Whether it's a bride's first dance roses or flowers from a grandmother's garden, I treat each project as if it were my own.

From wedding bouquets to childhood handprints, from temple art to holiday designs — each creation is a labor of love, designed to preserve the moments that matter most.

— Mel, Founder & Artist
